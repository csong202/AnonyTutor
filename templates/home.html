What is AnonyTutor?

AnonyTutor is an anonymous tutoring app! Students are given an auto-generated user name and matched with a tutor based on subject and curriculum.

Benefits of AnonyTutor:

  • AnonyTutor is easily accessible! All you have to do is download the app.
  • Students no longer feel embarassed or ashamed for asking for help.
  • Students who are afraid to speak up in class or approach their teacher now have a way to get help.
